Pokémon SAlt Crystal

Everything that changed with items.

Healing and Battle Items

  • Changed the amount of HP restored by most healing items, due to the new battle mechanics with level 50 stats.
  • Drinks (e.g. Fresh Water) are not usable in battle; they are cost-efficient healing on the field.
  • Berry Juice and Moomoo Milk are considered drinks.
  • Potions remain usable in battle, but are expensive when compared to drinks.
  • Berries have their modern names and grant relative HP healing (e.g. Oran Berry heals for 1/8 max. HP), and can be used as held items.
  • Rage Candy Bar is a cheap, field item that heals all status conditions.
  • Revival Herb revives for half HP.
  • Added a new item: Life Herb - Bitter herb that revives a Pokémon for its full HP.
  • Vitamins (e.g. Protein, Iron, etc.) are now consumable held items that boost their respective stat in battle.
  • New vitamins were added for missing stats: Zinc (Special Defense), Magnesium (Accuracy), Potassium (Evasion).
  • Leppa Berry restores 12 PP when the PP is 8 or less.
  • Elixer restores 24 PP, and Max. Elixer restores PP to full.
  • Type-boosting items (e.g. Charcoal, Magnet) boost moves for 20% damage rather than 10%.
  • X-Items (e.g. X-Attack) grant temporary stat boosts, to be on par with the new battle machacnics.

Poké-Marts

  • Changed prices for many items, and updated the inventories of many Poké-Marts.
  • The Goldenrod and Celadon department stores now sell Evolution stones.
  • Added a Berry market at the Berry house on Route 30, north of Cherrygrove City.

Other Changes

  • Improved the availability of many useful items.
  • Fixed various bugs regarding Kurt's Poké Balls.
  • The moves granted by many TMs and HMs were updated.
  • The only practical difference between a TM and an HM is that an HM is a reusable TM.
  • Thick Club and Light Ball can no longer make (Special) Attack wrap around above 1024.
  • Metal Powder can no longer increase damage taken with boosted (Special) Defense.
